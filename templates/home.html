Signs and Symptoms

  • Abnormal vaginal bleeding
  • Pelvic pain or pain during intercourse
  • Unusual discharge from the vagina
  • Discomfort during urination

Why is it important to get tested?

As stated by the World Health Organisation, early detection of cervical cancer greatly improves the chances of successful treatment. Regular screenings and tests can help identify precancerous conditions that may lead to cervical cancer.
